Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 125 g sugar
  • 450 g mascarpone
  • 300 mL cream
  • squeeze of lemon juice
  • plenty of coffee
  • plenty of alcohol
  • 1 package of lady fingers
  • chocolate for grating

Equipment

  • 1 large bowl
  • 1 small bowl
  • whisk

Procedure

  1. Separate two eggs, and put the yolks in a large bowl, whites in a small bowl
  2. Put half of the sugar in with the yolk and whisk to combine
  3. Slowly whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture
  4. Mix cream into yolk mixture until it's soft like whipped cream
  5. Add lemon juice to egg white, then whisk until soft and creamy
  6. Fold whites into yolk mixture
  7. Make a soaking mixture of coffee, sugar, and alcohol
  8. Assemble tiramisu, alternating a layer of cream and a layer of soaked lady fingers
  9. Grate chocolate on top of the last layer of cream
  10. Store in fridge until chilled and ready to eat
