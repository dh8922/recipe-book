Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 overripe bananas
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups AP flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

Prep

  1. Let eggs and butter get to room temperature

Equipment

  1. 9x5x3 loaf pan
  2. 1 large mixing bowl
  3. 2 medium mixing bowls

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â° F. Butter the loaf pan
  2. Cream sugar and butter in a large mixing bowl. Adds eggs one at a time, mixing between
  3. In a medium bowl, mash bananas with a fork. Mix in milk and cinnamon
  4. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  5. Add banana mixture to sugar mixture and combine
  6. Add flour mixture to sugar mixture and combine
    7. To Cook
  7. Pour batter into loaf pan and cook for 60-70 minutes, clean toothpick
  8. Cool on a rack for at least 15 minutes
  9. Wrap in foil and throw in the fridge for a few days
